Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We have the answer.

How do I find a tutor?

Simply search for the CITS unit you need help with and browse through our list of tutors. You can then select a tutor and book a session with them.

How do I become a tutor?

If you are interested in becoming a tutor, you can sign up on our website and create a tutor profile. Once your profile is approved, you can start tutoring students.

The cost of tutoring varies depending on the tutor and the subject. You can view the tutor's rates on their profile page.

How do I pay for tutoring?

You can pay for tutoring sessions using our secure payment system. Payments are made online, and you can choose from a variety of payment methods.
